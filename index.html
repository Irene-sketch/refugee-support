Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I apply for refugee aid?

A: You can visit our Law & Job Info Hub to find details on eligibility and application processes.

Q: What are my rights as a refugee?

A: As a refugee, you have the right to seek asylum, to be protected from being sent back to a country where you are at risk, and to have access to basic necessities like food, shelter, and healthcare.

Q: Who is eligible for resettlement?

A: Resettlement is generally offered to refugees who are in dire circumstances and who are unable to return to their home country or find a safe solution in another country. How do I apply for resettlement?

Q: How do I apply for resettlement?

A: You typically do not apply for resettlement directly, but rather, UNHCR or other organizations identify individuals who are in need of resettlement and refer them to resettlement countries.

About Us

"UMEED Refugee Support exists to build bridges between refugees and the resources they need to rebuild their lives. This website is meant to be a place to find necessary aid, get legal and job information, and to have a starting point for any questions that a refugee may have. By use of an AI chatbot, and by gathering crucial information, we are aiming to create a helpful space for those in need."
